Hierarchical Event Descriptors (HED)

Linking brain, body, behavior and experience in experiments through metadata to enable analysis

Why HED?

A very important question, for any neuroimaging time series data, is always, 'What exactly happened during this recording?
HED annotations record and store detailed answers to this question with the data to inform and facilitate data analysis.
